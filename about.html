ABOUT US

Established in 2018, KPS RETAILMART is rooted in the fertile heartland of Hooghly, West Bengal. With a deep commitment to quality and authenticity, we have emerged as a trusted name in the agro-processing industry, specializing in premium wheat products that are a staple in Indian households.

Our journey began with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers by delivering agro products that are unadulterated, nutritionally rich, and crafted with care. At KPS RETAILMART, we uphold the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and transparency—ensuring that every product reflects our dedication to purity, sustainability, and the well-being of our customers.

OUR HERITAGE OF TRUST

As a family-owned enterprise, we take immense pride in preserving traditional milling techniques passed down through generations, while seamlessly integrating modern food safety practices. Our state-of-the-art processing unit in Binodbati, Hooghly operates under strict FSSAI guidelines, ensuring every grain of wheat that enters our facility transforms into products that meet stringent quality benchmarks.

From carefully selecting the finest wheat from local farms to meticulous processing and packaging, we maintain complete control over our supply chain to guarantee purity in every packet.

Our Product Philosophy

At KPS RETAILMART, we believe food should be as nature intended – pure, potent, and packed with nourishment. That’s why we:

  • Reject shortcuts : No artificial additives, bleaching agents, or preservatives
  • Honor tradition : Stone-ground techniques for optimal nutrient retention
  • Prioritize hygiene : ISO-certified processes from cleaning to packaging
  • Support local : Direct partnerships with Hooghly farmers for fresh harvests

Beyond Business – Our Commitment

We recognize our responsibility towards:

  • Farmers : Fair pricing and timely procurement to support agricultural communities
  • Consumers : Affordable nutrition without compromising on quality
  • Environment : Sustainable practices like water recycling and waste reduction

Looking Ahead

While we cherish our roots, we continuously innovate to meet evolving consumer needs. From introducing fortified variants to expanding our distribution network across Eastern India, we remain committed to our core values of quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

Every spoonful of our Atta, Maida, Sooji and Bran Flakes carries the legacy of our land and the promise of your family’s health. We invite you to experience the KPS difference – where tradition meets trust in every grain.
