ABOUT US

Established in 2018, KPS RETAILMART is rooted in the fertile heartland of Hooghly, West Bengal. With a deep commitment to quality and authenticity, we have emerged as a trusted name in the agro-processing industry, specializing in premium wheat products that are a staple in Indian households. Our journey began with a clear vision: to bridge the gap between local farmers and consumers by delivering agro products that are unadulterated, nutritionally rich, and crafted with care.

At KPS RETAILMART, we uphold the highest standards of quality, hygiene, and transparency—ensuring that every product reflects our dedication to purity, sustainability, and the well-being of our customers.

Introduction of KPS Group

Founded in the 1940s by Mr. Kalipada Saha, the KPS Group began its journey in the fertilizer industry in the Bengal province. The group has since grown into a diverse conglomerate, building a reputation for trust over more than eight decades.

  • 1950s: The company officially registered and diversified into rice-based products.
  • 1960s: KPS entered the film industry with its first cinema hall in 1967.
  • 1970s: The first company became a private limited company, and the group expanded into the cold storage business in different places.

Over the last 20 years, the KPS Group has made several significant strides:

  • 2007: Started its first Modernized Rice mill, named K P Rice Mill, at Dhania Khali.
  • 2011: accrued second rice mill namely Jasmine Rice Mill.
  • 2014: Entered the education sector with first educational venture stared with a Poly technic college at BelmuriDhaniakhali . There after We started Pharmacy College at same premises.
  • 2018: Began managing 4 Nos government ITI college with PP model at Hooghly district.
  • Post-COVID: Opened the KPS mall at Chinsurah Hooghly. And convert Conventional Cinema Halls into shopping malls at Dhaniakhali.
  • 2025: Opened KPS Retailmart Private Limited for marketing and distribution of FMCG products
  • The Group is also constructing the first Private university in the Hooghly District with the vision to start the campus by the end of 2026.

KPS Agro Products

KPS Agro Products was officially formed on June 19, 2020, with production beginning on April 17, 2023. The company serves major clients, including ITC, Britannia, Biskfarm, Sona, Jaya, Mio Amore. Apnar Mart, Super Market and Kaventar

The group is also preparing to launch its own brand, Kitchen King, which will produce small pouch-packaged goods. The brand was conceptualized in 2020 and registered in 2024.

OUR HERITAGE OF TRUST

As a family-owned enterprise, we take immense pride in preserving traditional milling techniques passed down through generations, while seamlessly integrating modern food safety practices. Our state-of-the-art processing unit in Binodbati, Hooghly operates under strict FSSAI guidelines, ensuring every grain of wheat that enters our facility transforms into products that meet stringent quality benchmarks. From carefully selecting the finest wheat from local farms to meticulous processing and packaging, we maintain complete control over our supply chain to guarantee purity in every packet.

Our Product Philosophy

At KPS RETAILMART, we believe food should be as nature intended – pure, potent, and packed with nourishment. That’s why we:

  • Reject shortcuts : No artificial additives, bleaching agents, or preservatives
  • Honor tradition : Stone-ground techniques for optimal nutrient retention
  • Prioritize hygiene : ISO-certified processes from cleaning to packaging
  • Support local : Direct partnerships with Hooghly farmers for fresh harvests

Beyond Business – Our Commitment

We recognize our responsibility towards:

  • Farmers : Fair pricing and timely procurement to support agricultural communities
  • Consumers : Affordable nutrition without compromising on quality
  • Environment : Sustainable practices like water recycling and waste reduction

Looking Ahead

While we cherish our roots, we continuously innovate to meet evolving consumer needs. From introducing fortified variants to expanding our distribution network across Eastern India, we remain committed to our core values of quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction. Every spoonful of our Atta, Maida, Sooji and other upcoming products carries the legacy of our land and the promise of your family’s health. We invite you to experience the KPS difference – where tradition meets trust in every grain.
