ABOUT

Rooted in the fertile lands of Hooghly, West Bengal, KPS RETAILMART has been a trusted name in the agro-processing industry since 2018. We specialize in crafting premium-quality wheat products that form the foundation of countless Indian kitchens. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision – to bridge the gap between local farmers and households by delivering authentic, unadulterated, and nutritionally rich agro products while upholding the highest standards of quality and hygiene.
